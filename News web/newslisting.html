  • 633 Indian students died abroad in 5 years; Canada, US saw most deaths

In the last five years, 633 Indian students have died abroad due to various reasons, including accidents and violent attacks, according to the Ministry of External Affairs. Canada saw the highest number of deaths at 172, followed by the US with 108. Canada also saw most deaths due to attacks.
